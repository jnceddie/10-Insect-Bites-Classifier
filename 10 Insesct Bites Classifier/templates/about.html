üî¨ Methodology

Data Collection & Preparation

The system was trained on a comprehensive dataset of bug bite images, covering 10 different types of insect bites. The dataset includes thousands of images with proper labeling and annotation.

Model Architecture

Base Architecture: EfficientNetV2B0 - A state-of-the-art efficient convolutional neural network

Why EfficientNetV2B0?

  • Optimal balance between accuracy and inference speed
  • Improved training efficiency with progressive learning
  • Pre-trained on ImageNet for transfer learning
  • Optimized for both mobile and desktop deployment
  • 7.1M parameters with improved scaling and robustness

Innovation: Pattern-Aware Architecture with automatic feature extraction (38 features)

Input: 224√ó224 RGB images

Output: 12-class probability distribution (10 bug types + no_bites + other_skin_condition)

Training Process

  • Strong data augmentation (rotation, flip, brightness, contrast)
  • Label smoothing for better generalization
  • Class weight balancing
  • Two-stage training (head + fine-tuning)
  • Cosine decay learning rate schedule
